## MVP Features List (with HMRC & Companies House automation)

## 1. Expense Management
- Add an expense (amount, date, category, supplier, notes)
- Select from HMRC-approved expense categories
- Upload receipts (photo/PDF/image)
- Google Drive storage for receipts
- Mark expense as reimbursable or company-paid
- VAT breakdown (if VAT-registered)
- Search, filter, and export expenses

## 2. Allowable Expense Guidance
- HMRC-aligned expense categories with descriptions
- Category allowability guidance (fully/partially/disallowable)
- Smart category suggestions when entering expenses
- Warnings for likely non-allowable expenses

## 3. Bookkeeping Tools
- Profit & loss overview
- Expenses by category breakdown
- Monthly financial summary
- Simple cashflow tracker
- Annual accounts CSV export
- Year-end summary preparation for CT600

## 4. Compliance & Deadlines
- Automatic calculation of key company milestones:
  - Confirmation Statement due date (Companies House)
  - Annual Accounts filing deadline (Companies House)
  - Corporation Tax return (CT600) due date (HMRC)
  - Corporation Tax payment due date (HMRC)
- Dashboard showing upcoming deadlines
- Email and push notification reminders for each milestone
- Year-end bookkeeping checklist
- General compliance checklist

## 5. Document Management
- Upload and store company documents (Incorporation certificate, contracts, HMRC letters)
- Google Drive integration for file storage
- Searchable document library
- Document tagging (Accounts, HMRC, Legal, Receipts, etc.)

## 6. Tax Preparation Tools
- Corporation tax estimator (based on profit and allowable expenses)
- Dividend calculator
- Accountant-ready summary export (CSV/PDF)
- Reports formatted for CT600 submission

## 7. HMRC & Companies House Automation
- Generate **Annual Accounts PDF** (micro-entity template)
- Generate **Confirmation Statement summary** (for Companies House)
- Generate **CT600-ready summary reports** for HMRC
- Export all filings as **downloadable PDFs or CSVs** for submission
- Prepare reports in a **future MTD-compliant digital format** for HMRC

## 8. Authentication & Security
- Email/password or OAuth login
- Two-factor authentication
- Encrypted storage

## 9. Settings & Integrations
- Company profile setup (name, registered address, company number, financial year)
- Google Drive integration
- Email notification settings
- Data export/backup functionality

## 10. Quality-of-Life Features
- Mobile-first UI
- Dark mode
- Dashboard overview of company health and finances
